# Svitrigaila: Relationships

## Kin
*   **Jagelis**: He resents his brother's authority and frequently rebels against him.
*   **Skirgaila**: Fears and hates him. Skirgaila often treats him like a child.
*   **Julianna of Tver**: His mother. She favored him, which likely fueled his sense of entitlement.

## Allies (Temporary)
*   **The Teutonic Knights**: Frequently seeks their aid when in exile.
*   **The Orthodox Lords**: They support him against Polish-Catholic influence.

## Enemies
*   **Vytis**: A rival for the leadership of those who oppose Polish influence.
