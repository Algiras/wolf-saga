# Book II: Auksinis Šešėlis (The Golden Shadow) - Detailed Outline

## Part 1: The Imperial Dream (1387–1395)
1.  **Stepių Vėjas (Steppe Wind)**: Introduction to the Golden Horde's fall. Tokhtamysh arrives as a fugitive.
2.  **Juodasis Krantas (The Black Coast)**: Vytis begins building the stone castles on the Black Sea.
3.  **Kijevo Šešėliai (Shadows of Kyiv)**: Vytis consolidates the East. Interactions with the Rus' boyars.
4.  **Auksinė Moneta (The Golden Coin)**: The influence of the **Gold-Glutton**. The prosperity and corruption of the new administration.
5.  **Dvi Karūnos (Two Crowns)**: The tension between Vytautas and Jogaila (now in Kraków). The "Cold War" continues.
6.  **Skirgailos Kerštas (Skirgaila's Revenge)**: Skirgaila's growing madness and his secret deals with the neighbors.
7.  **Svitrigailos Maištas (Svitrigaila's Rebellion)**: The introduction of the youngest, most volatile brother.
8.  **Vorsklos Žinia (Message from Vorskla)**: The alliance is proposed. Vytis sees a chance to rule the East and West.
9.  **Geležinis Maršas (The Iron March)**: A coalition army (Lithuanians, Poles, Teutons, Tatars) assembles.
10. **Vilko Ambicija (The Wolf's Ambition)**: Vytis ignores the omens and the warnings of the **Root-Mother**.

## Part 2: The Horizon of Blood (1395–1399)
11. **Raudonas Dangus (Red Sky)**: The march into the Steppe. The landscape becomes alien, endless, and hostile.
13. **Edigu (The Steppe Butcher)**: First encounter with the Mongol tactical genius.
14. **Velniavos Sapnas (The Devil's Dream)**: **Velnias** shows Vytis a vision of himself crowned on the Mongol throne.
15. **Dulkės ir Kraujas (Dust and Blood)**: The beginning of the Battle of the Vorskla River.
16. **Lūžusi Strėlė (The Broken Arrow)**: The tactical disaster. The heavy armor fails in the heat and dust.
17. **Skerdynės prie Upės (Slaughter at the River)**: The total collapse. 74 princes of Lithuania die in a single day.
18. **Vyties Bėgimas II (The Flight II)**: Vytis narrowly escapes again. This time, there is no mother's fog, only shame.
19. **Stepės Badas (The Steppe-Hunger)**: **Erdene-Chuluu** feeds on the fallen army.
20. **Sugrįžimas į Pelenuosius Trakus (Return to Ashen Trakai)**: Vytis arrives back as a defeated man, his dream of Empire shattered.

## Part 3: The Hard Lessons (1399–1401)
21. **Šaltas Pripažinimas (Cold Recognition)**: Vytis has to beg Jogaila for help to stabilize the realm.
22. **Vilniaus Susitarimas (The Vilnius Pact)**: A new, more humble agreement between the cousins.
23. **Skirgailos Pabaiga (The End of Skirgaila)**: The mysterious death (poison?) of the rival brother.
24. **Žemaitijos Šauksmas (The Cry of Samogitia)**: The uprisings begin. The **Iron Saint**'s response is brutal.
25. **Pasienio Gaisrai (Border Fires)**: The Knights push back, feeling Vytis's weakness.
26. **Onos Išmintis (Ona's Wisdom)**: Ona helps Vytis pivot from brute force to diplomacy.
27. **Eglės Sugrįžimas (Eglė's Return)**: A warning from the forest — the Green Wood is dying.
28. **Nauja Sąjunga (A New Alliance)**: Building the foundations for the union with Poland.
29. **Geležinio Vilko Atgimimas (Rebirth of the Iron Wolf)**: Vytis discards the "Golden" dream and prepares for the "Green" war.
30. **Epilogas (Epilogue)**: High on the Malbork walls, the Grand Master looks at the map of Samogitia. "Next time, we finish them."
