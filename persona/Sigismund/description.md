# Sigismund of Luxemburg (The Golden Emperor)

**Title**: Holy Roman Emperor, King of Hungary, Bohemia, and Germany
**Era**: Book V (The Invisible Crown)
**Archetype**: The Manipulator / The Grand Strategist

## Appearance
*   Tall, with a meticulously groomed forked beard.
*   Always dressed in gold-threaded velvet. He smells of exotic incense and expensive wine.
*   Possesses a magnetic, actor-like presence.

## Personality
*   **Charming & Deceptive**: He is the ultimate diplomat. He uses people's ambitions against them.
*   **Visionary**: He wants a united Europe under his rule, primarily to fight the "Infidel" Turks.
*   **Opportunistic**: He offered Vytautas the crown primarily to break the Polish-Lithuanian union, which he saw as a threat to his own power.

## Motivation
*   **Chaos**: To keep his neighbors fighting each other so he remains the ultimate arbiter.
*   **Defense of Christendom**: Truly fears the Ottoman expansion and seeks allies.
