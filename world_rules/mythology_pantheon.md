# Mythology & Pantheon

## The Major Gods (Adored & Feared)

*   **Dievas (The Elder)**: The distant sky god. Ruler of order and peace. Often seen as inactive in human affairs.
*   **Perkūnas (The Thunderer)**: The warrior god of storms, lightning, and justice. The most worshipped deity by soldiers and dukes. Weapon: The Axe/Hammer.
*   **Velnias (The Trickster)**: God of the underworld/dead and animals. Not purely evil, but chaotic. Teaches magic, grants wealth, but demands a price. Often tricks humans.
*   **Žemyna (Mother Earth)**: Goddess of fertility and the land. All oaths sworn on the earth are binding.
*   **Saulė (The Sun)**: The mother of life. The tears of Saulė are amber (*gintaras*).
*   **Laima (The Weaver)**: Goddess of fate. She weaves the thread of every human life.

## Creatures & Spirits

*   **Laumės**: Fairy-like women with bird features. They live near water/swamps. Can bless or curse children. Weavers of the sky.
*   **Ragana**: Witches/Seers. They control the forces of nature, healing, and curses. They gather on hilltops.
*   **Aitvaras**: A household spirit (fiery rooster/dragon). Brings wealth (stolen from neighbors) to those who feed it omelets.
*   **Žaltys (The Grass Snake)**: Sacred serpent. Killing one brings doom to the household. Symbol of health and fertility.
*   **Vilkakis**: Werewolves. Warriors who can shift shape, often associated with the Iron Wolf legend.

## Cosmology

*   **The World Tree**: Roots in the Underworld (Velnias), Trunk in the Human Realm, Branches in the Sky (Dievas/Perkūnas).
*   **The Iron Wolf**: A dream/prophecy symbol of the founding of the great capital (Vilnius). "A wolf made of iron, howling with the voices of a hundred wolves."
