# Anna: Timeline

## Era: The Escape
1.  **The Hostage**: After Vytis's arrest, she is under house arrest but allowed to visit him.
2.  **The Plan**: Smuggles in tools and clothes over weeks.
3.  **The Swap**: Distracts the guards while Vytis changes. Stays behind to delay discovery.
4.  **The Flight**: Escapes shortly after (or is ransomed/released when Jagelis realizes Vytis is gone). Joins Vytis in exile.
