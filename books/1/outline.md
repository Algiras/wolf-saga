# Story Outline: Book I - The Wolf's Exile

## Theme
"Brother against Brother." The struggle between the Old Gods (Paganism) and the New Faith (Christianity), mirrored by the conflict between the honorable warrior (Vytautas) and the pragmatic politician (Jogaila).

## Plot Arc

### Part 1: The Fall
*   **Prologue**: *The Sacred Grove*. The High Priestess foresees the "Two Wolves devouring the moon."
*   **Chapter 1**: *The Old Duke*. Kęstutis (The Uncle) believes he has secured the realm. He trusts his nephew Jogaila.
*   **Chapter 2**: *The Trap*. Jogaila invites Kęstutis and his son Vytautas to a peace counsel. They are seized.
*   **Chapter 3**: *The Tower*. Imprisoned in Kreva Castle. Kęstutis is found dead (strangled). Vytautas realizes he is next.

### Part 2: The Escape
*   **Chapter 4**: *The Handmaiden*. Vytautas's wife, Ona, visits the dungeon. A plan is hatched. Vytautas swaps clothes with a handmaiden to escape.
*   **Chapter 5**: *The Flight*. A desperate chase through the wilderness. Vytautas reaches the border of the Iron Order (Teutonic Knights).
*   **Chapter 6**: *The Devil's Bargain*. Vytautas promises the Grand Master he will convert if they help him retake his throne.

### Part 3: The Return
*   **Chapter 7**: *The Iron Army*. Vytautas marches back into Lithuania with the German knights. The people are torn: their hero returns, but with their greatest enemy.
*   **Chapter 8**: *The Siege of Vilnius*. A brutal battle. Vytautas nearly takes the capital but realizes the Iron Knights want to burn it, not save it.
*   **Chapter 9**: *The Choice*. Vytautas turns on his "allies" or brokers a secret truce with Jogaila to save the city.
