# Birute: Timeline

1.  **The Vow**: Becomes a priestess at Palanga, guarding the sacred fire.
2.  **The Abduction**: Kestus takes her by force after she refuses him.
3.  **The Matriarch**: Rules Trakai alongside Kestus for decades.
4.  **The End**: Dies in 1382. Some say she was drowned on Skirgaila's orders; others say she died of grief.
5.  **The Cult**: Her hill at Palanga becomes a place of pilgrimage.
