# Jagelis (The Shadow King)

**True Name**: Jogaila
**Title**: Grand Duke of Lithuania, King of Poland
**Age**: 30
**Archetype**: The Politician / The Modernizer

## Appearance
*   Slender and somewhat frail-looking, but with restless energy.
*   Dark, intelligent eyes that never settle.
*   Dresses in fine velvets and silks (Western style), contrasting with his cousins' furs and leather.
*   Clean-shaven, unlike the bearded pagan dukes.

## Personality
*   **Calculated**: Every move is a chess piece. He doesn't act on emotion; he acts on gain.
*   **Paranoid**: He sees treachery everywhere, largely because he is treacherous himself.
*   **Visionary**: He believes the Old Ways are dying. To save Lithuania, he believes it must become Christian/Western, even if it means destroying its soul.

## Motivation
*   **Power**: To unite Lithuania and Poland into a superpower.
*   **Legacy**: To be the founder of a new dynasty that rivals the Holy Roman Empire.
*   **Fear**: He fears the Teutonic Order and believes only conversion will stop them.
