# Key Personas (Dramatis Personae)

## House of the Wolf (The Gediminids)

### **Grand Duke Mindaugas II (The Unifier)**
*   *Archetype*: Tywin Lannister meets King Ecbert.
*   *Goal*: To forge a Kingdom from tribes.
*   *Conflict*: Pagan vs. Christian. Tradition vs. Survival.
*   *Fate*: Betrayed by his own blood.

### **Duke Kęstutis (The Old Boar)**
*   *Archetype*: Ned Stark. Honorable, warrior, pagan.
*   *Role*: Defender of the West.
*   *Relationship*: Loyal brother to Algirdas, suspicious of Jogaila.

### **Duke Algirdas (The Silent Conqueror)**
*   *Archetype*: Stannis Baratheon (but successful). Stern, expansionist.
*   *Role*: Expands the realm East.
*   *Relationship*: Trusts only Kęstutis.

### **Jogaila (The Shadow King)**
*   *Archetype*: Littlefinger/Renly mix. Ambitious, pragmatic, willing to convert for power.
*   *Goal*: The Polish Crown.
*   *Conflict*: Rivalry with Vytautas.

### **Vytautas (The Great Wolf)**
*   *Archetype*: Jon Snow / Robb Stark. The true hero who is forced into exile and returns.
*   *Goal*: Independence for Lithuania.
*   *Arc*: Escape from prison, alliance with enemies, victory at the Great Battle.

## The Antagonists

### **Grand Master of the Iron Order**
*   *Goal*: Conversion or extermination of the "Last Pagans of Europe".
*   *Methods*: Crusades, raids, political manipulation.

## The Mystics

### **The HPC (High Priestess of the Grove)**
*   *Role*: Keeper of the Eternal Fire (*Amžinoji Ugnis*). Interpreter of Perkūnas's will.
*   *Political Power*: Can sway the common people against the Dukes.
