# Julianna: Timeline

1.  **The Match**: Marries the pagan giant Algirdas, bringing Rus' influence to the capital.
2.  **The Succession**: Orchestrates Jogaila's ascent over his older half-brothers.
3.  **The Negotiator**: Tries to marry Jogaila to a Moscow princess.
4.  **The Twilight**: Sees her Eastern plans fail as Jogaila turns toward Poland.
5.  **The End**: Dies in 1391, buried with Orthodox honors.
