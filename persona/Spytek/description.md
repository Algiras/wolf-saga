# Spytek of Melsztyn (The Hussite Knight)

**Title**: Knight-Magnate of the Crown
**Era**: Book V
**Archetype**: The Rebel Noble / The Progressive Warrior

## Appearance
*   Dashing and charismatic. He wears the latest Italian-style plate armor.
*   Possesses an open, friendly face that masks a fierce intellect.

## Personality
*   **Idealistic**: He is drawn to the Hussite movement's calls for reform and equality.
*   **Rivalrous**: He despises the power of the Church (led by Olesnicki) and sees the corruption in the Polish clergy.
*   **Allied to the East**: Unlike Olesnicki, he favors a more equal partnership with Lithuania.

## Motivation
*   **Reform**: Breaking the power of the magnate oligarchy.
*   **Liberty**: Supporting the rights of the lesser nobility and the religious freedom of the Hussites.
