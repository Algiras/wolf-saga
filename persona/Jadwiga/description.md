# Jadwiga (The Saint-King)

**True Name**: Jadwiga Rex (King Jadwiga)
**Title**: King of Poland, Duchess of Lithuania
**Age**: 12 (at marriage) / 25 (at death)
**Archetype**: The Sacrifice / The Holy Diplomat

## Appearance
*   Ethereal and delicate. Reddish-gold hair.
*   Dressed in the height of Western European fashionâ€”elaborate crowns, silks, and pearls.
*   Always carries a small black crucifix.

## Personality
*   **Wise beyond years**: Crowned "King" at 10, she understood power while still a child.
*   **Deeply Pious**: She believes her marriage to the "Barbarian" Jogaila is a mission from God to save the souls of Lithuania.
*   **Melancholic**: She had to abandon her true love (William of Austria) for the good of her nation.

## Motivation
*   **The Mission**: The Christianization of the North.
*   **Charity**: Building schools, helping the poor, refounding universities.
*   **Peace**: Balancing the hot tempers of Jogaila and Vytis.
