# Book I: The Silent Pact - Expanded Structure

## Part 1: The Fall (The Last Days of Peace)
1.  **Senasis Šernas (The Old Boar)**: Trakai Castle. Kęstutis receives the envoys. He feels safe.
2.  **Vilko Perspėjimas (The Wolf's Warning)**: Vytis argues with his father. Shows evidence of Teutonic movements near the border.
3.  **Gintaro Sapnas (The Amber Dream)**: Birutė visits the sacred grove. She sees the "Black Ship." The first interaction with the **Amber Queen**.
4.  **Kelionė į Vilnių (Journey to Vilnius)**: The ducal retinue moves. Foreboding signs on the road. The **Dievas Senelis** (Beggar) appears.
5.  **Dvigubas Veidas (The Double Face)**: Jogaila's perspective. He is nervous. **The Shadow-Seller** whispers courage (or coldness) into his ear.
6.  **Geležiniai Vartai (The Iron Gates)**: Arrival in Vilnius. The trap springs. The arrest.
7.  **Tylos Puota (The Feast of Silence)**: Skirgaila secures the city. The massacre of Kęstutis's loyal guard.
8.  **Krėvos Požemiai (The Dungeons of Kreva)**: Interrogation. Kęstutis refuses to abdicate.
9.  **Paskutinė Giesmė (The Last Song)**: The murder of Kęstutis. A mystical strangulation.
10. **Šaltas Rytas (Cold Morning)**: Vytis discovers the truth. He realizes he is dead if he stays.

## Part 2: The Escape (Survival)
11. **Našlė (The Widow)**: Birutė learns of her husband's death. She refuses to weep and prepares a ritual.
12. **Gedulo Skraistė (The Shroud of Mourning)**: Ona (Anna) arrives at Kreva. She plays the "silly grieving woman" to fool the guards.
13. **Keitimas (The Swap)**: The dungeon visit. The high-tension scene of swapping clothes.
14. **Šešėlis Naktyje (Shadow in the Night)**: Vytis walks out of the castle disguised as a handmaiden. The **Amber Queen**'s fog helps him.
15. **Pabėgimas (The Flight)**: Vytis rides until his horse dies. He is hunted by Skirgaila's trackers.
16. **Pelkės Vaikai (Children of the Swamp)**: Vytis is saved by **Eglė** and the swamp spirits who mislead the pursuers.
17. **Pasienis (The Border)**: Reaching the Teutonic lands. Vytis is starving and desperate.
18. **Geležinis Magistras (The Iron Master)**: Introduction to Grand Master Winrich von Kniprode. The clash of ideologies.
19. **Velnio Sandėris (The Devil's Bargain)**: Vytis kneels. He promises to convert. The **Iron Saint** laughs in the firelight.

## Part 3: The Return (War of the Cousins)
20. **Svetimi Dievai (Foreign Gods)**: Vytis returns with the Teutonic army. He sees his homeland burning—by his own allies.
21. **Pirmoji Kova (First Blood)**: Vytis leads a charge against his own cousin's forces. The psychological toll.
22. **Vilniaus Apgultis I (Siege of Vilnius I)**: The bombardment. The walls of the capital shake.
23. **Ugnis ir Pelenai (Fire and Ash)**: The Crusaders plan to sack the city completely. Vytis realizes he cannot let this happen.
24. **Pasirinkimas (The Choice)**: Vytis sabotages a Teutonic siege engine or sends a warning to the defenders.
25. **Išdavikų Išdavikas (Traitor of Traitors)**: The Order realizes Vytis is playing them. He has to escape *again* or turn the army around.
26. **Susitikimas Peleninėje (Meeting in the Ashes)**: A secret parley with Jogaila. No forgiveness, but a temporary truce.
27. **Gintaro Auka (The Amber Sacrifice)**: Birutė's final act to seal the pact or protect her son (mystical death/disappearance).
28. **Nauja Tvarka (The New Order)**: The cousins agree to share power... for now.
29. **Krikštas (The Baptism)**: Hints of the coming Christianization.
30. **Epilogas (Epilogue)**: Skirgaila watches from the shadows, juggling three black balls. The game is not over.
