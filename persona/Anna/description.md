# Anna (The Duchess)

**True Name**: Ona
**Title**: Duchess of Trakai
**Age**: 25
**Archetype**: The Clever Survivor / The Partner

## Appearance
*   Fair hair and sharp, observant blue eyes.
*   Deceptive fragility; she looks like a court lady but has the stamina of a soldier.
*   Modest dress, but always carries a hidden dagger.

## Personality
*   **Cunning**: She navigates court politics better than her husband. She sees traps before they are sprung.
*   **Devoted**: Her loyalty to Vytis is absolute, but she is not afraid to challenge him when he is wrong.
*   **Brave**: Walking into a dungeon to save her husband required nerves of steel.

## Motivation
*   **Family**: To protect her husband and children (who are held hostage).
*   **Survival**: She knows that if Vytis falls, she is dead.
