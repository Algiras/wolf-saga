# Book IV: Nematoma Karūna (The Invisible Crown) - Detailed Outline

## Part 1: The Golden Cage (1411–1425)
1.  **Pokario Tyla (Post-War Silence)**: The quiet years. Rebuilding the cities and the administration.
2.  **Karaliaus Šešėlis (The King's Shadow)**: Vytautas's influence across Europe grows. He is the "Kingmaker."
3.  **Horošto Taryba (The Council of Constance)**: Lithuania's delegation faces the intellectual wrath of the Order.
4.  **Olesnickio Iškilimas (The Rise of Olesnicki)**: The introduction of the master manipulator. The **Gold-Glutton**'s true champion.
5.  **Jogailos Senatvė (Jogaila's Old Age)**: The King is tired, but his heirs (Sofia's sons) create a new crisis.
6.  **Vytis ir Sūnus? (Vytis and the Son?)**: The recurring tragedy of Vytautas having no male heir.
7.  **Lucko Suvažiavimas (The Congress of Lutsk)**: The grandest event in Eastern Europe. The world comes to Vytautas.
8.  **Imperatoriaus Pasiūlymas (The Emperor's Offer)**: Sigismund of Luxembourg offers Vytautas a Crown.
9.  **Lenkų Baimė (The Polish Fear)**: The prospect of an independent Lithuanian Kingdom creates panic in Kraków.
10. **Auksinis Smauglys Šypsosi (The Gold-Glutton Smiles)**: Money begins to change hands to stop the coronation.

## Part 2: The Stolen Dream (1425–1429)
11. **Karūnos Kelionė (The Crown's Journey)**: The masterpiece of European goldsmiths is traveling through the Empire.
12. **Šnipų Karas (War of Spies)**: Olesnicki's agents versus Vytautas's scouts. A shadow fight in the borderlands.
13. **Vartų Užrakinimas (Locking the Gates)**: The Polish border is closed. The crown is intercepted.
14. **Vyties Įniršis (The Rage of Vytis)**: The realization that his "allies" have stolen his destiny.
15. **Henne Žaidimai (Henne's Games)**: **Jester Henne** appears in the Polish court, juggling three black balls.
16. **Nuvilti Trakai (Disappointed Trakai)**: The empty throne room. The psychological collapse of the aging hero.
17. **Jogailos Dilema (Jogaila's Dilemma)**: He wants to help his cousin, but he is trapped by his own lords.
18. **Geležinio Vilko Paskutinis Staugimas (The Iron Wolf's Last Howl)**: Vytis gathers his last strength for a final political push.
19. **Karūnos Ieškotojai (The Crown-Hunters)**: A desperate, secret mission to find and recover the stolen crown.
20. **Tušti Delnai (Empty Palms)**: The crown has vanished—or was never there?

## Part 3: The Sunset (1429–1430)
21. **Vyties Nuovargis (The Fatigue of Vytis)**: The body begins to fail. The mind wanders back to the miško šnabždesiai.
22. **Paskutinis Susitikimas (The Final Meeting)**: Jogaila and Vytautas, two old men, looking at the same map one last time.
23. **Svitrigailos Šešėlis (Svitrigaila's Shadow)**: The volatile brother prepares to seize the power Vytis is leaving.
24. **Onos Mirtis (The Death of Ona)**: The loss of the only person who truly understood him.
25. **Trakų Tyla (Silence of Trakai)**: The castle feels haunted by the ghosts of Kęstutis and Birutė.
26. **Šaknų Motinos Atsisveikinimas (The Root-Mother's Farewell)**: The forest begins to encroachment on the castle walls.
27. **Paskutinė Malda (The Final Prayer)**: Vytis at the altar, but his heart is in the grove.
28. **Vilko Mirtis (The Death of the Wolf)**: October 1430. The fall from a horse—or a quiet fading away?
29. **Nematoma Karūna (The Invisible Crown)**: The crown arrives... but a day too late. Or perhaps it was always invisible.
30. **Epilogas (Epilogue)**: Jester Henne tosses the three black balls into the Trakai lake. "The play is done. The myth remains."
