# Ulrich von Jungingen (The Iron Sentinel)

**Title**: 26th Grand Master of the Teutonic Order
**Era**: Book IV (The Green Wood)
**Archetype**: The Zealot / The Aggressor

## Appearance
*   A mountain of a man, even in his 50s.
*   His white cloak with the black cross is always immaculate, even in battle.
*   Eyes that shine with a dangerous, religious fervor.

## Personality
*   **Arrogant**: He believes the Order is invincible and "favored by the Virgin."
*   **Aggressive**: Unlike his cautious predecessors, he seeks a final, decisive battle to crush the "Pagan-Polish hydra."
*   **Honorable (In his own way)**: He fought to the last at Grunwald, refusing to flee even when the battle was lost.

## Motivation
*   **Purity**: To finish the mission of the Order and claim the "Wild Lands" for Christ.
*   **Dominance**: To prove the superiority of the German Knights over the "Eastern Rabble."
