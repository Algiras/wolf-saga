# Vytis: Timeline

## Era: The Civil War
1.  **The Peaceful Summer**: Vytis rules Trakai, supporting his father Kestus and uncle Algirdas.
2.  **The Betrayal**: Attends the peace counsel at Kreva. Arrested by Jagelis's guards.
3.  **The Dungeon**: Imprisoned in the dark tower. Hears the screams of his father dying in the cell next door.
4.  **The Escape**: Swaps clothes with Anna's handmaiden. Climbs down the castle wall in the dead of night.
5.  **The Exile**: Flees through the forest, hunted by Skirgaila's men. Reaches the Teutonic fortress of Marienburg.
6.  **The Pact**: Kisses the cross (falsely) to gain the support of the Grand Master.
7.  **The Return**: Marches on Vilnius with a German army. The Siege of Vilnius begins.
