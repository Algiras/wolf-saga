# Historical Timeline (Adapted)

## Era 1: The Rise of the Wolf King (Mid-13th Century)
*Based on King Mindaugas*

*   **The Unification**: The realm is fragmented into warring tribes. **Mindaugas**, a ruthless chieftain, unites them through blood and marriage.
*   **The False Conversion**: To stop the **Teutonic Order** (The Iron Knights), Mindaugas accepts Christianity and is crowned King.
*   **The Assassination**: Mindaugas is assassinated by his own kin/rivals, plunging the realm back into chaos and paganism.

## Era 2: The Brothers' Pact (Mid-14th Century)
*Based on Grand Dukes Algirdas and Kęstutis*

*   **The Diarchy**: Two brothers rule together. **Algirdas** expands East (The Steppes, The Rus lands), while **Kęstutis** defends the West against the Iron Knights.
*   **The Great Expansion**: The Duchy becomes the largest state in the continent, stretching from sea to sea.

## Era 3: The Crown of Thorns (Late 14th Century)
*Based on Jogaila and Vytautas*

*   **The Succession Crisis**: Algirdas dies, leaving the throne to his son **Jogaila**. Tensions rise between Jogaila and his uncle Kęstutis.
*   **The Betrayal**: Jogaila captures and imprisons Kęstutis, who dies under suspicious circumstances (murdered?).
*   **The Escape**: Kęstutis's son, **Vytautas**, escapes captivity and seeks aid from the Iron Knights (the enemy) to reclaim his birthright.
*   **The Civil War**: A brutal war between cousins, shifting alliances, and burning castles.

## Era 4: The Great Battle (Early 15th Century)
*Based on the Battle of Grunwald*

*   **Reconciliation**: Jogaila (now King of a neighboring powerful kingdom via marriage) and Vytautas reconcile to face the common enemy.
*   **The Battle of the Green Woods**: A massive coalition army led by Vytautas and Jogaila crushes the Iron Knights, breaking their power forever.
