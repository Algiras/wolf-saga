# Skirgaila (The Iron Hand)

**True Name**: Ivan / Skirgaila
**Title**: Duke of Trakai (Polockas), Regent of Lithuania
**Age**: 32
**Archetype**: The Enforcer / The Machiavellian Brother

## Appearance
*   Strong, martial bearing. Often seen in full plate armor, even when off the field.
*   Cold, calculating expression. He has the "look of a man who knows where the bodies are buriedâ€”because he put them there."
*   Prominent scar across his knuckles.

## Personality
*   **Brutal & Loyal**: He is Jogaila's shadow. He does the things Jogaila cannot or will not do.
*   **Ruthless**: Suspected of the murder of his uncle Kestus. He believes that the survival of the dynasty justifies any crime.
*   **Hard-Drinking**: Known for intense bouts of drinking followed by bouts of explosive violence or deep depression.

## Motivation
*   **Security**: Ensuring Jogaila's throne is absolute.
*   **Power**: He enjoys the exercise of authority and the fear he inspires.
