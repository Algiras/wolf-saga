# Birute (The Priestess-Queen)

**True Name**: BirutÄ—
**Title**: Grand Duchess of Lithuania, High Priestess (Legendary)
**Age**: Deceased (during the main Civil War) / 50s (at time of death)
**Archetype**: The Forbidden Love / The Symbol of Faith

## Appearance
*   Hauntingly beautiful in youth. Long, white-blonde hair.
*   In maturity, she possessed a "terrible grace."
*   Always associated with the Amber Hill and the Eternal Fire.

## Personality
*   **Spiritual**: Truly believed in her vows to the gods.
*   **Unendingly Loyal**: Once she accepted Kestus, she became his most steadfast supporter, even if their marriage began in blood.
*   **Mystical**: Known to have visions and a deep connection to nature.

## Motivation
*   **Protection of the Sacred**: Guarding the spiritual heart of Lithuania from the Cross.
*   **The Mother**: Ensuring Vytis inherits his father's strength but not his father's fate.
