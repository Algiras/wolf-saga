# Jagelis: Timeline

## Era: The Civil War
1.  **The Succession**: Inherits the Grand Ducal title from his father, Algirdas.
2.  **The Secret Treaty**: Signs the Union of Krewo, promising to convert for the Polish crown.
3.  **The Coup**: Lures Kestus and Vytis to a trap. Arrests them.
4.  **The Coronation**: Is baptized and crowned King of Poland in Krakow.
5.  **The Rebellion**: Faces the uprising led by the escaped Vytis and his Teutonic allies.
