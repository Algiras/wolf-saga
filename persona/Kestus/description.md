# Kestus (The Old Boar)

**True Name**: Kęstutis
**Title**: Duke of Trakai, Defender of Samogitia
**Age**: 80 (approx)
**Archetype**: The Old Guard / The Tragic Hero

## Appearance
*   White hair and huge white beard, braided in the old style.
*   Massive frame, though stooped with age.
*   Covered in scars from a lifetime of fighting Crusaders.
*   Wears heavy bear furs and iron amulets of Perkūnas.

## Personality
*   **Stubborn**: Refuses to abandon the Old Gods. "I was born under the oak, and I will die under the oak."
*   **Blunt**: Has no patience for politics or lies.
*   **Loyal**: loved his brother Algirdas and trusted his nephew Jagelis—a fatal mistake.

## Motivation
*   **Defense**: To keep the Teutonic Knights out of Samogitia.
*   **Tradition**: To preserve the Romuva faith against the encroaching Christian tide.
