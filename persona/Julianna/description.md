# Julianna (The Dowager Wolf)

**True Name**: Uliana Alexandrovna of Tver
**Title**: Grand Duchess of Lithuania, Dowager Duchess
**Age**: 50s (at the height of the drama)
**Archetype**: The Matriarch / The Chessmaster

## Appearance
*   Dignified and regal. Her face is a map of decades of political survival.
*   Always wears Orthodox icons hidden beneath Lithuanian pagan patterns.
*   Highly literate, often found with scrolls in a court of warriors.

## Personality
*   **Calculating**: She managed the transition of power from Algirdas to Jogaila.
*   **Devout (Secretly)**: While she accepted a pagan court, she pushed her children toward Orthodoxy and the East.
*   **Pragmatic**: She cares about the dynasty, not the gods.

## Motivation
*   **Dynasty**: Ensuring the sons of her line (Jogaila's line) rule above all others.
*   **Eastward Shift**: Pulling Lithuania away from the Teutonic West and toward the Rus' principalities.
