# Book III: Žaliasis Miškas (The Green Wood) - Detailed Outline

## Part 1: The Gathering Storm (1401–1409)
1.  **Sušalę Pumpurai (Frozen Buds)**: Samogitia is handed over again, but the people refuse to submit.
2.  **Vyties Diplomatija (Vytis's Diplomacy)**: Vytis begins a secret campaign of letters and gold across Europe.
3.  **Žemaitijos Sukilimas (The Samogitian Uprising)**: The peasants rise. **Eglė** leads the "invisible" resistance.
4.  **Malborko Šešėliai (Shadows of Malbork)**: The new Grand Master, Ulrich von Jungingen, prepares a total crusade.
5.  **Karaliaus Žodis (The King's Word)**: Jogaila faces pressure from the Polish lords (the **Gold-Glutton**).
6.  **Slaptas Susitikimas (The Secret Parley)**: Vytis and Jogaila meet in a hunting lodge to plan the impossible.
7.  **Geležies Sunkumas (The Weight of Iron)**: The Order's propaganda depicts the Lithuanians as beasts.
8.  **Šešėlių Pardavėjas Sugrįžta (The Shadow-Seller Returns)**: Jene offers Vytis a "shortcut" to victory that requires another kinsman's blood.
9.  **Vytis Atsisako (The Refusal)**: Vytis chooses the hard road over the "easy" deal.
10. **Ašarų Upė (River of Tears)**: The Order begins a scorched earth policy in the borderlands.

## Part 2: The Green Labyrinth (1409–1410)
11. **Mobilizacija (The Gathering)**: The Polish-Lithuanian host assembles. A mix of banners, faiths, and languages.
12. **Tiltas per Vyslą (The Bridge over the Vistula)**: The secret engineering feat that allows the army to cross in one night.
13. **Miško Šnabždesiai (Forest Whispers)**: **Eglė** and the Serpent-Kin guide the scouts through the "impassable" woods.
14. **Geležinis Šventasis Pabunda (The Iron Saint Awakes)**: The Teutonic host moves out with a terrifying, religious fervor.
15. **Dulukų Laukas (The Field of Grunwald)**: The two armies finally face each other under a blazing July sun.
16. **Tyla prieš Audrą (Silence before the Storm)**: The psychological warfare begins. The "Two Swords" incident.
17. **Lietuvių Užtais (The Lithuanian Charge)**: The initial suicide-like charge of the Light Cavalry.
18. **Traukimasis? (The Retreat?)**: The famous (or infamous) tactical withdrawal that baits the knights.
19. **Miško Kerštas (The Forest's Revenge)**: The Polish reserves and the returned Lithuanians surround the Order.
20. **Geležies Mirtis (The Death of Iron)**: Ulrich von Jungingen falls. The **Iron Saint**'s elite are slaughtered.

## Part 3: The Price of Peace (1410–1411)
21. **Kruvini Laužai (Bloody Campfires)**: The night after the battle. The realization of the scale of the carnage.
22. **Malborko Apgultis (Siege of Malbork)**: The failure to take the main castle. Vytis's fatigue or hidden agenda?
23. **Torūnės Taika (The Peace of Thorn)**: The diplomatic results are less than the military victory.
24. **Žemaitijos Laisvė (Liberty of Samogitia)**: The land is finally returned to the Duchy.
25. **Laimėtojų Nuovargis (The Winners' Fatigue)**: Internal friction between the allies.
26. **Jogailos Sielvartas (Jogaila's Grief)**: The burden of the Crown and the loss of his old life.
27. **Vytis ir Trakai (Vytis and Trakai)**: Returning to a peaceful home, but feeling like a stranger.
28. **Nauji Veidai (New Faces)**: The rise of the younger generation (Sigismund, Olesnicki).
29. **Šaknies Motinos Padėka (The Root-Mother's Thanks)**: A vision of the forest regrowing over the graves.
30. **Epilogas (Epilogue)**: A young Polish priest (Olesnicki) looks at the maps. "The sword did its work. Now the ink must do ours."
